Creamy Avocado Bowl

Easy ⏱ 20 min Lunch High Protein Gluten Free
480
kcal
Calories
18g
Protein
52g
Carbs
24g
Fat
  • Avocado
    1 large
  • Brown rice
    1 cup cooked
  • Cherry tomatoes
    1 cup
  • Cucumber
    1/2
  • Chickpeas
    1/2 cup
  • Tahini
    2 tbsp
  • Lemon juice
    1 tbsp
  1. Cook brown rice according to package instructions and let cool slightly.
  2. Halve the avocado, remove pit, and slice into thin pieces.
  3. Halve cherry tomatoes and slice the cucumber.
  4. Drain and rinse chickpeas from the can.
  5. Mix tahini with lemon juice, a pinch of salt, and 2 tbsp water to make dressing.
  6. Assemble the bowl: rice at the bottom, then arrange all toppings. Drizzle with tahini dressing.
